The 6-Week Mastery Plan
Week 1: The Foundations (Where we are right now) - Type hinting, Pydantic basics, routing, and HTTP methods.

Week 2: Core Logic & Structure - Error handling, dependency injection, and structuring your app with APIRouter.

Week 3: Clean Architecture & Databases - Setting up SQLAlchemy, connecting PostgreSQL/SQLite, and understanding the Service and CRUD layers.

Week 4: Security & Authentication - Implementing JWTs, hashing passwords, and locking down routes using OAuth2.

Week 5: Advanced Mechanics - Asynchronous programming (async/await), Background Tasks, and WebSockets.

Week 6: Production & DevOps - Unit testing with Pytest, Dockerizing the app, and final deployment preparations.
